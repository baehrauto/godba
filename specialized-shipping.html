Value-Added Services

Scheduled Delivery Appointments

Schedule delivery appointments that work with your timeline. We coordinate delivery times in advance to ensure your shipment arrives when it's most convenient for you, minimizing wait times and ensuring someone is available to receive your delivery.

Flexible Scheduling

Work with our team to arrange delivery appointments that fit your schedule. Whether you need morning, afternoon, or specific time windows, we coordinate to meet your availability and ensure smooth delivery coordination.

Hazmat Shipping

Expert handling and compliance for hazardous materials shipments. Our certified team ensures proper classification, documentation, labeling, and packaging according to DOT, IATA, and IMDG regulations. We handle all hazmat requirements so your dangerous goods ship safely and legally.

Battery Shipping

Specialized handling for lithium batteries and battery-powered devices. We ensure compliance with UN 3480, UN 3481, and UN 3090 regulations for air and ground transport. Our team handles proper packaging, documentation, and labeling requirements for all battery types including lithium-ion, lithium metal, and other rechargeable batteries.

LTL Recovery

Expert Recovery

Specialized expertise in locating and recovering lost or delayed LTL shipments. We know where to look and how to get your cargo moving again.

Dedicated Tracking

Continuous monitoring and tracking of your shipment throughout the recovery process. Real-time updates and proactive communication keep you informed.

Rapid Resolution

Fast response times and efficient recovery processes. We work quickly to locate your shipment and get it back on track to minimize delays.

LTL Recovery Service Options

01

Lost Shipment Recovery

Expert services to locate and recover lost LTL shipments. Comprehensive tracking and investigation to find your missing cargo and get it delivered.

02

Delayed Shipment Resolution

Proactive intervention for delayed LTL shipments. We identify bottlenecks, coordinate with carriers, and expedite delivery to get your cargo moving.

03

Communication & Updates

Dedicated communication throughout the recovery process. Regular updates, status reports, and direct contact with your recovery coordinator.

Carrier Coordination

Direct coordination with LTL carriers to locate shipments and expedite delivery. Established relationships for faster resolution.

Investigation Services

Thorough investigation to track down missing shipments. We follow every lead and use our network to locate your cargo.

Expedited Delivery

Priority handling once your shipment is located. We expedite the delivery process to minimize further delays.
