Why Choose Ocean Shipping?

Cost-Effective

Most economical option for large volume shipments. Ocean freight offers significant cost savings for non-urgent cargo.

Full Container & LCL

Flexible options from full container loads to less-than-container-load. We match the right solution to your shipment size.

Port-to-Port & Door-to-Door

Complete logistics solutions from port handling to final destination delivery. We handle every step of the journey.

Service Options

01

Full Container Load (FCL)

Dedicated container for your shipment. Ideal for large volume cargo with maximum security and control.

02

Less Than Container Load (LCL)

Share container space for smaller shipments. Cost-effective solution for partial loads with flexible scheduling.

03

Door-to-Door Service

Complete end-to-end service from origin pickup to final destination delivery. One seamless solution.

Import Services

Complete import handling with customs clearance and inland delivery to your facility.

Export Services

Expert export documentation and port handling for shipments leaving the country.

Customs Clearance

Professional customs brokerage services to ensure smooth clearance and compliance.
