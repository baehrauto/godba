Mission-Critical Healthcare Logistics

In healthcare, delays aren't just inconvenient—they can be life-threatening. DBA specializes in time-sensitive medical logistics, from organ transport to temperature-controlled pharmaceuticals, ensuring every shipment arrives exactly when and where it's needed.

With over 25 years of experience in healthcare logistics, we understand the unique challenges of medical shipping: strict temperature requirements, regulatory compliance, and the absolute necessity of on-time delivery.

24/7 Emergency Response

Round-the-clock availability for urgent medical shipments that can't wait.

Temperature-Controlled Transport

Specialized handling for pharmaceuticals and biologics requiring precise temperature control.

Regulatory Compliance

Full compliance with FDA, DEA, and international healthcare regulations.

What We Ship

From life-saving organs to critical pharmaceuticals, we handle the most time-sensitive healthcare shipments.

Organ Transport

Time-critical organ transport with specialized handling and real-time tracking to ensure safe, timely delivery.

Pharmaceuticals

Temperature-controlled shipping for vaccines, biologics, and prescription medications requiring strict environmental controls.

Medical Equipment

Urgent delivery of surgical instruments, diagnostic equipment, and life-support systems to hospitals and clinics.
